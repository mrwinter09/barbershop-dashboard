The primary action control across the ClipperTakes platform; pick the variant by emphasis and surface.

```jsx
<Button variant="primary">Explore the Stories</Button>
<Button variant="accent" size="lg">Tell your story</Button>
<Button variant="outline">Recommend someone</Button>
<Button variant="ghost" size="sm">Read more</Button>
<Button variant="on-dark">Get involved</Button>
```

- **variant**: `primary` (deep petrol, the default cultural CTA) · `accent` (Rotterdam yellow + ink, loud invitations) · `outline` (coffee hairline, fills on hover) · `ghost` (quiet text action) · `on-dark` (paper, for use over petrol/photo panels).
- **size**: `sm` (36px) · `md` (44px, default) · `lg` (52px).
- `fullWidth` stretches the button; `leftIcon`/`rightIcon` accept any node (pair with Lucide SVGs); `as="a"` renders a link.
- Microcopy is editorial and invitational — "Tell your story", never "Book now" or "Save appointment".
