Identity chip for a participant — portrait when available, initials otherwise.

```jsx
<Avatar name="Fatima El Amrani" />
<Avatar name="Ivan Winter" tone="petrol" size="lg" />
<Avatar src="/portraits/sanne.jpg" name="Sanne" size="sm" />
```

- **size**: `xs | sm | md | lg`. **tone**: `neutral | petrol | clay | moss` (fallback background when no portrait).
