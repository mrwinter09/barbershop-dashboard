ReflectionItem renders one quiet, guestbook-style response under a story.

```jsx
<ReflectionItem
  text="I grew up two streets from here. This is exactly how I remember the market."
  name="Sanne"
  neighborhood="Afrikaanderwijk"
  date="14 MAR 2026"
/>
```

- Stack several inside the story page's reflections section (they self-divide with a hairline).
- Keep text short — a sentence or two. No avatars, likes, or reply threads by design.
